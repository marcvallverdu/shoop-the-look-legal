Privacy Policy

Shop the Look · Effective date: February 11, 2026

Shop the Look ("we", "our", "the app") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

  • Photos you submit. When you analyze an outfit, the image is sent to our servers and third-party AI services (Google Gemini, Google Lens) for clothing detection and product matching. We do not store your photos after processing is complete.
  • Purchase & subscription data. If you subscribe to a premium plan, payment is handled by Apple (In-App Purchase) and RevenueCat. We receive a pseudonymous user ID and subscription status — not your payment details.
  • Device identifiers. We may collect anonymous device IDs for analytics (crash reports, usage statistics).
  • Usage data. Screens viewed, features used, and interaction events to improve the app experience.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To analyze outfit photos and return product matches.
  • To manage your subscription and unlock premium features.
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google (Gemini AI, Google Lens) — image analysis
  • RevenueCat — subscription management
  • Apple — In-App Purchases, App Store

4. Data Retention

Photos are processed in real-time and not stored beyond the analysis session. Subscription data is retained as long as your account is active. Analytics data is aggregated and anonymized.

5. Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete the app at any time to stop all data collection.
  • Request deletion of any stored data by contacting us.
  • If you are in the EU/UK, you have rights under GDPR including access, rectification, and erasure.

6. Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
