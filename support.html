Common Questions

How does it work?

Take or pick a photo of any outfit. Our AI identifies individual clothing items and finds matching products from online stores, including prices and available deals.

What photos work best?

Clear, well-lit photos where clothing items are visible. Works with selfies, street style photos, screenshots from Instagram/TikTok, and fashion editorials.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Go to Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Shop the Look → Cancel.

My analysis didn't find good matches

Try a clearer photo with better lighting, or crop to focus on a specific item. Very unique or vintage items may have fewer matches available.
